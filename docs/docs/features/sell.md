# Sell List & Valuation

Two money-out views for your collection: a working **sell list** and a printable **valuation
report**.

## Sell list

The **Sell** tab lists every card you've flagged to sell, priced with a running total.

- **Flagging** — tag a card `for-sale` (also `for sale` / `sale`) from its
  [card page](cards.md#tags) or in bulk from [search results](../search/syntax.md).
- The tab shows quantity, price each, and line total, sorted most-valuable first, with a grand
  total.
- **Export** — download the list as **CSV** (with a totals row) or **text** from the tab.

It mirrors the [trade binder](trade.md), but where trade shows your *surplus*, the sell list is
whatever you've explicitly flagged.

## Valuation report

A printable summary for insurance or records, at **`/valuation`** (linked from the Stats tab).

- **Totals** — estimated value, total cards, distinct printings, and distinct cards.
- **Value by rarity** and **top sets by value**.
- **Top cards by value** with per-copy and total value.
- Dated, with a print button (`Ctrl/Cmd+P` also works).

!!! note "Graded cards"
    A [graded card's](grading.md) manual value override replaces its market price in the valuation
    report — so slabs are valued at what you say they're worth.
